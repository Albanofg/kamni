The difference

Most strategy advisors have studied this industry. KMV Advisory is built by someone who has run it.

The practice is grounded in a career that has carried global P&Ls from $100M to more than $2B, guided a company to a successful exit from the CEO's chair, and championed more than half a dozen acquisitions across Genomics, Diagnostics, and Life Science Tools. Much of that career was spent building and scaling the instrument, reagents and consumables, informatics, and service businesses at the heart of Life Science Tools, the recurring-revenue model that turns a single product into a business that compounds. When an engagement calls for more, the practice draws on a small circle of trusted specialists who have operated at the same level, so the depth on hand always fits the problem. That operating experience sits behind every engagement, with respect for how hard this work is and for the fact that the people who carry the plan forward will be your own. That experience spans North America, Europe, and Asia, where the same technology often meets very different commercial, regulatory, and reimbursement realities.
